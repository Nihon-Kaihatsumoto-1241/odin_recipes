Omurice

Description

A Japanese style omelette with fried rice

Ingredients

  • 1 chicken breast, diced into 0.5cm cubes
  • 8 medium eggs
  • 1 medium onion, diced finely
  • 4 cups of cooked white rice
  • 2 cups of ketchup

Steps

  1. Wash and rinse rice under running water.
  2. Cook rice in a rice cooker.
  3. Dice onions finely.
  4. Fry onions with a neutral flavoured oil until slightly browned.
  5. Take onions off pan. Fry diced chicken until cooked/slightly browned.
  6. Reduce heat. Add onions back in and add tomato sauce and let it cook for about a minute.
  7. Add rice to the mixture and set aside.
  8. Heat fry pan, add a little oil and place about two beaten eggs on the pan.
  9. Immediatley place about a cup of the rice mixture in the middle the egg whilst cooking - roughly in an American football shape.
  10. Fold up the wings of the egg over the rice football.
  11. When cooked, take a large plate and flip the egg and mixture onto the plate so that the rice is on the bottom and egg is on top.
  12. You can use a paper towel to shape the egg.
  13. Decorate the egg with ketchup.
  14. Eat with spoon!
