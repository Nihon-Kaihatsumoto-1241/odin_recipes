Onigiri

Description

A quick, easy, ready to go Japanese favorite!

Ingredients

  • 4 cups Sushi rice
  • 3 small tins of Sandwich Tuna
  • Kewpie mayonnaise
  • Nori sheets
  • Salt

Equipment

  • Rice cooker
  • Triangular onigiri container
  • Plastic cling wrap

Steps

  1. Wash and rinse rice under running water.
  2. Cook rice in a rice cooker.
  3. Whilst the rice is cooking, strain sandwich tuna in a medium size sieve to remove oil. Use a tablespoon to squeeze out the oil.
  4. Place tuna in a bowl and add Kewpie mayonnaise until it is a consistency that you prefer.
  5. Fluff the rice with a rice ladle after the rice is cooked.
  6. Place plastic cling wrap into onigiri container.
  7. Add salt to the cling wrap.
  8. Half fill container with rice.
  9. Place a tablespoon of tuna mayonnaise mix on rice and flatten out.
  10. Fill the remaining half of the container, shaping it into a triangle.
  11. Take a nori sheet and place on table.
  12. Place triangle rice 'ball' on the middle of the top edge of the nori sheet, so that most of the nori sheet covers onigiri but only a small part will poke out.
  13. Fold the nori sheet, wrapping it around the rice 'ball'.
  14. Eat and enjoy!
