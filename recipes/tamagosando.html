Tamago Sando

Description

A quick yummy dish

Ingredients

  • 4 boiled eggs
  • Kewpie mayonnaise
  • White sandwich bread

Steps

  1. Make boiled eggs.
  2. Mash eggs with a fork. Roughly if you like chunks, finely if you want a smooth paste.
  3. Add Kewpie mayonnaise until it is a consistency that you prefer.
  4. Spread the mix between two slices of bread.
  5. Eat!
